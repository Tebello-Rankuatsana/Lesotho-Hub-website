📈 Marketing 📈

1.Know Your Market - Grow crops or raise livestock that are in demand in your local area.
2.Add value - Instead of just selling raw produce, consider processing foods instead.
3.Have a Social Media presence - In a world where most people have cellular devices, its imperative that you stay in their faces all day everyday.

💻 Technology & Innovation 💻

1.Use modern tools - Make use of things drones to help manage a farm better.
2.Automation - Invest in machinery that will not only increase productivity, it will vastly decrease the cost you could incur, in addition, robots don't rest so you can do much more in less time.
3.Be Flexible - Make sure you're regularly improving your repetoire in terms of skills, so you don't fade out in this fast moving industry

💧 Asset(land,resources etc.) Management 💧

1.Soil Quality - regularly test your soil and use Organic matter or crop rotation to maintain facility.
2.Use water smart - Install self automated irrigation systems to save water and cut costs.
3.Prevent erosion - Plant cover crops, build contours to protect your soil.

🤝 Community & Sustainability 🤝

1.Collaborate with other farmers - Share equipment, buy imports , sell exports, or join cooperatives.
2.Educate - Invest in sharpen the skills of your self and your workers.
3.Have foresight - Adopt sustainable practices to keep your farm productive for future generations.
